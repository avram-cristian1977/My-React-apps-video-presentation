X-mas Organizer

Winter is coming...X-mas included. This app can help you to organize you gifting plans by storing for you the X-mas surprizes for your family and friends.
You can make a budget for eachother, you can change the gift status (found status, bought status or sent status) therefore you will track your plan better!
The app will always show you the remaining time until Christmas by visiting homepage.
I used React, redux (toolkit), firebase (auth and storing), react-elastic-carousel, EmailJS, Corel, etc.
